# 1ge (1 Tenge Together) 🇰🇿✨
> **"Turning Micro-cents into Global Change. One Tenge at a time."**

## 🌐 Vision
**1ge** is not just a donation app; it is a **social fintech ecosystem** designed to redefine how humanity improves the world. In a future where technology often divides us, 1ge uses it to unite. Our goal is to make social impact a "default settings" for every citizen on the planet.

We believe that no contribution is too small. If 19 million people contribute just **1 Tenge**, we generate 19 million Tenge in a single day. That is the power of unity.

## 🚀 The Concept: "Cyber-Altruism"
Inspired by the aesthetics of futuristic sci-fi where technology serves a higher purpose, 1ge introduces a new way of social interaction:

* **Crowdsourced Urban Projects:** Users propose real-world improvements for their cities (playgrounds, hospitals, social cafes). When a project is funded and built, every donor can say: **"I built this."**
* **Visual Social Status (Gamification):** Every user has a digital "glow." As you contribute and level up, your profile evolves. Imagine sitting in a cafe at night, and your phone pulses with a specific light—a silent signal to others that you are part of the movement. We compete in **usefulness**, not just consumption.
* **Direct Support Marketplace:** Donations are funneled directly to families in need. They can purchase essentials (books, school uniforms, food) through our integrated marketplace, ensuring 100% transparency and zero waste.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Architecture
* **Frontend:** Built with HTML5/CSS3/JavaScript, currently migrating to **React** for high-performance UI, fluid animations (Framer Motion), and dynamic state management.
* **Backend:** Powered by **Firebase** (Authentication & Firestore) for real-time data sync.
* **AI Layer:** Utilizing **OpenAI / Gemini API** via a secure Cloudflare/Node.js proxy to:
    * Automate transparent financial reporting.
    * Moderate and categorize community-driven urban projects.
    * Generate personalized impact stories for donors.
